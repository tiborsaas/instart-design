Printing technology and color accuracy

Our printing partner uses state of the art, direct to garment printing technology which results in a great variety of designs that can be printed on various products.

Due to differences in customers' computer monitor setup (how the colors are calibrated) the print may differ slightly from what is presented on the Site. Some colors may come out slightly darker or brighter or small details might blend together. Coloring can change between devices and display settings.

By placing an order, you agree that you acknowledge this and products attempted to be returned for such reasons may not be eligible for a return. We guarantee a high-quality product, however nuances in colors are not up to our control, as every individual may have a different device they view their prospective order on.

The ink used is water based and eco-friendly. This makes the feel much softer than traditional screen printed shirts, but just as durable. They use CPSIA (Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act) compliant inks, which are required when printing on children's wear for ages 12 and below.
